Testing

The idea is to test it by seeing how durable and noticeable it is. I will also see reviews and stuff. I want to see if I should improve the design for more fashion wanting people.

Evaluation

Some problems are that the shoes are made of paper, and will not last very long, making them a waste of money. They will also cause a risk of paper cuts, and have so many ways to easily get destroyed unintentionally, like fire or water or rips/tears.

Future Improvements

This shoe is better than the original for many reasons. It is more shoe-shaped, and it is sturdier. This shoe will last a lot longer than other shoes and can be painted without its main shape really changing. It also has a functioning tongue, and it can be made in multiple different sizes. It can also be more water resistant if it is coated with acrylic paint. The shoe will hold much more strongly and be more protective, with only being made of wood and cardboard.

Impact

Economy

Good for the environmental state of the world. This is because it is made of paper and wood. Wood is biodegradable because it comes from trees, and paper also comes from trees.

Well Being

This is extremely cheap because it is made of paper and wood. Paper and wood come from trees and are accessible all around the world (almost). Trees can grow where there are good environments, so as long as it is not too cold (just Antarctica really) and it is not in the ocean (with some exceptions) and it is not in the desert and not underground, it should be fine. That seems limited, but those conditions for trees also apply to where people can live.
